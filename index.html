Our History

Lightning Labs was founded in 2010 by a team of scientists and engineers who shared a passion for improving lightning safety and technology. Since then, we have grown into a global leader in lightning solutions, serving clients in over 50 countries.

Our mission is to provide innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions that enhance the safety and efficiency of businesses and communities worldwide.
